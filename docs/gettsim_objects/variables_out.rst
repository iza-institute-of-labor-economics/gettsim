.. _variables:

Basic input variables
=====================

The table below gives an overview of typical variables selected by users to simulate
with GETTSIM. You can simulate them by defining them as target in
:func:`compute_taxes_and_transfers`.


+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
| Varianöe          | Explanation                                                    |
|                   |                                                                |
+===================+================================================================+
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
| rentenv_beitr_m   | Monthly amount employee old-age pensions contributions         |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
| arbeitsl_v_beitr_m| Monthly amount employee unempl. insurance contributions        |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
| ges_krankenv_beitr_m | Monthly amount employee health insurance contributions         |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
| pflegev_beitr_m    | Monthly amount of long term care insurance                     |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
| arbeitsl_geld_m   | Monthly amount of unemployment assistance                      |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
| rente_anspr_m     | Monthly amount of old-age pensions                             |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
| entgeltpunkte_update     | Updated earning points for pension claim                       |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
| abgelt_st_tu       | Monthly capital income tax due, individual                     |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
| soli_st_tu         | Monthly solidarity surcharge due, individual                   |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
| kindergeld_m      | Monthly child Benefit, individual                              |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
| eink_st_tu         | Income Tax per tax unit                                   |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
| eink_st_m_tu      | Monthly income Tax Due, couple sum                             |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
| unterhaltsvors_m  | Alimony advance payment, individual                            |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
| regelsatz_m       | Household socio-economic *need*, lump-sum                      |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
| kinderzuschlag_m  | Monthly additional child benefit, household sum                |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
| elterngeld_m      | Monthly elterngeld, individual                                 |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
| wohngeld_m_hh     | Monthly housing benefit, household sum
|
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
| arbeitsl_geld_2_m | Monthly social assistance, household sum                       |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
| verfügb_eink_m    | Monthly disposable income, individual                          |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
| verfügb_eink_hh_m | Monthly disposable income including household level benefits   |
|                   | ,household sum                                                 |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------+
